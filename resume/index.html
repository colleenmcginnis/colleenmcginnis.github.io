ABOUT

I am passionate about making the world a better place through design. With a dual background in architecture and environmental science, I often work at the intersection of urban environmental and social systems. My approach, combining empathetic human-centered design and hard technical skills, allows me contribute to projects with visual design, program design, or the design of the built environment.

As a graduate student in the Master of Science in Sustainable Design program at the University of Texas at Austin, I conducted research in both public interest design and biomimicry [built systems that mimic natural systems]. My masters thesis focused on the potential for a new solar energy system designed to mimic the multiple scales of the photosynthetic processes found in natural ecosystems.
