--- layout: default title: Sliding Display subtitle: Delta Institute, 2015 category: data visualization ---

Project Overview: The sliding display was initially developed as an analog way for conference attendees to interact with one of Delta Institute’s complex projects. The display illustrates how Delta’s multilayered process builds to a final result.

Installation: The flexible design allows for content to be easily exchanged as there are opportunities to showcase new work.

Interaction: The design encourages people to physically interact with the content, and it proved to be an effective conversation-starter among conference attendees.
