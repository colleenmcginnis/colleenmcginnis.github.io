Project Overview: The DeltaLumin Narrative site outlines the approach, implementation, and evaluation of a three month pilot product and service aimed at illuminating energy use for low-income Chicago residents. The site was designed and developed as a part of General Assembly's Front-End Web Development course. Visit the site at deltalumin.com/year-one-report.
