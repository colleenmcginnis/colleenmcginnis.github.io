Bachelor Hall Atrium

Miami University, 2009

SUSTAINABLE DESIGN

Project Overview: The purpose of this project was to design a poetic and environmentally efficient atrium space in the current courtyard of bachelor hall. This home to the English and Math departments lacks space to come together as a community of learners.


Concept: The design concept was the use of pragmatic solutions cloaked in poetic experience. The major elements of the design come from responses to environmental conditions, but the way in which the conditions were adressed respond to the poetic nature of the site.
