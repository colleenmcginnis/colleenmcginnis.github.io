Clifton Branch Library

Miami University, 2009

SUSTAINABLE DESIGN

Project Overview: This project was my first informal introduction to sustainable design. When working to develop a concept, a professor asked what I would most like to achieve with this project - sustainability - and he encouraged me to let sustainable strategies dictate aesthetic and spatial decisions.


Form: The form was designed as a reaction to typical wind patterns to increase the effectiveness of natural ventilation during warm months.


Facade: The facade was designed as a reaction to sun patterns throughout the year including a shading system consisting of horiztonal and vertical static louvers.
