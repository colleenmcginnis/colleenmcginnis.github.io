SUSTAINABLE DESIGN

Project Overview: This project was my first informal introduction to sustainable design. When struggling to articulate a concept around which to focus my design, a professor asked what I would most like to achieve with this project - sustainability - and he encouraged me to let sustainable strategies dictate aesthetic and spatial decisions.
